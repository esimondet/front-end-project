Hero Maps

Hello Agent,

We need you to assemble a team of heroes! The S.H.I.E.L.D. database will help you learn about available heroes, you'll need this information if you're to track them down. With each hero we've recorded their last known positions. It'll take your best detective work to track them down!

Use this interactive site to learn about your favorite Marvel superheroes! If you come across a hero that doesn't have a bio, you can always clear your hero roster and start fresh!
